# Kamal Coffee — Brand Guidelines

## What Kamal Coffee is

Kamal Coffee is a **premium Vietnamese ready-to-drink iced coffee** brand. The product comes in a can. The can is the hero. The product is the hero.

The website should feel like a **premium beverage advertisement** — cinematic, warm, restrained — not a generic ecommerce store.

## Core product truths

These facts are non-negotiable and should appear consistently across the site:

1. **Vietnamese iced coffee** — authentic in spirit, premium in presentation
2. **Vegan**
3. **Dairy free**
4. **Sweetened with allulose instead of sugar**

## Brand personality

| Attribute | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| Warm | Cream, oat, sand tones; soft daylight; natural materials |
| Premium | Editorial photography, generous whitespace, restrained type |
| Restrained | Few words, one idea per section, no clutter |
| Cinematic | Product shots feel like film stills, not catalog thumbnails |
| Product-first | The can leads every key moment |
| Local-first | Farmers markets in LA and Orange County, not national retail |

## Visual direction: warm minimalism

### Color palette

Use cream, oat, sand, taupe, espresso brown, caramel, muted navy, and charcoal. See [design-tokens.md](design-tokens.md) for exact values.

### Surfaces and texture

- Matte finishes over glossy UI chrome
- Natural materials in photography (linen, stone, wood, soft light)
- Avoid cold grays, neon accents, and high-contrast "tech startup" palettes

### Photography

- **Editorial product photography** — the can is always the focal point
- Soft, directional daylight; warm shadows
- Minimal props; nothing that competes with the product
- No stock-photo energy, no obvious AI-generated imagery

### Typography

- Restrained: one or two type families maximum
- Strong hierarchy with few weights (regular + one accent weight)
- Generous letter-spacing on small labels; tight tracking on headlines
- Avoid decorative or overly trendy display fonts

## Voice and tone

- **Confident, not loud** — let the product speak
- **Clear, not clever** — one question answered per section
- **Warm, not casual** — premium without being stiff
- **Honest, not hypey** — no fake claims, no invented availability

### Words to prefer

- Vietnamese iced coffee, ready-to-drink, craft, allulose, dairy-free, vegan, farmers market, Los Angeles, Orange County

### Words to avoid

- Shop now, add to cart, best seller, limited time, nationwide, available at [retailer], subscribe and save, guilt-free (overused), artisanal (overused without context)

## What the site should feel like

A visitor should leave remembering:

1. The **Kamal Coffee can**
2. That it is **Vietnamese iced coffee**, **vegan**, **dairy-free**, and **sweetened with allulose**
3. That they can **find Kamal at local farmers markets** in LA and OC

## What the site should NOT feel like

- A Shopify template
- A SaaS landing page
- A DTC supplement brand with urgency timers
- A coffee blog with long unbroken paragraphs
- An AI-generated "modern minimalist" site with generic gradients and lorem ipsum

## Homepage section philosophy

Every section answers **exactly one question**. Planned structure:

| Section | Question it answers |
|---------|---------------------|
| Hero | What is Kamal? |
| Product intro | What makes it special? |
| Allulose | What is allulose? |
| Dairy-free / vegan | Is it dairy-free and vegan? |
| Vietnamese coffee | What is Vietnamese coffee? |
| Our Story preview | Who is behind Kamal? |
| Find Us | Where can I find Kamal? |
| FAQ | What else should I know? |
| Footer | How do I stay in touch? |
