## 3. Allulose — What is allulose?

**Question:** What is allulose?

**Headline (draft):** Sweetened with allulose, not sugar.

**Body (draft):** Allulose is a naturally occurring sweetener found in foods like figs and raisins. It delivers sweetness without the sugar — so you get the flavor you expect, with a lighter touch.

**Notes:**
- Educational, not medical. Avoid health claims.
- Keep it simple — most visitors will not know what allulose is.
